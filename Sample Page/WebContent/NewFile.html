Affordable, professional mono printers for the busy office

If you need a black & white printer that supports the highest levels of office productivity – without straining your budget – you need the B412dn. Built for speed and maximum efficiency, it produces the first printed page in less than 5 seconds and the rest at an impressive 33 pages per minute. And that’s just the start.

Choose the B412dn for fast individual printing or for sharing on the office network saving costs without losing productivity.

Designed for productivity

Of course, there’s more to productivity than fast print speeds. The true 1200 x 1200dpi resolution delivers fine, crisp detail which is generously supported by a powerful 667MHz processor and 512MB of memory, and a paper supply that can be increased to 880 sheets to mirror your productivity demands. There’s even a 100-sheet multi-purpose tray, supporting a range of paper sizes, to meet everyday office printing requirements. Plus, it comes with OKI Template Manager – making it easy for you to create and print a wide variety of office documents.

Maximum Efficiency

The compact design of the B412dn means it can be placed on any desktop within your office. What’s more, its high yield toner lets you print up to 7,000 pages without replenishment, helping to keep user interventions and running costs down. Other essential features include automatic double sided printing as standard, a ‘Power Save’ mode that reduces energy consumption to just 7W, and because the toner cartridge and drum are separate, you get maximum use out of all consumables.

The B412dn also comes with our popular PrintSuperVision software – the smart way to actively manage your office printing, for improved control by tracking and monitoring usage of your networked printers.

Built for endurance

Maximum reliability means maximum return on investment. And that’s what the B412dn delivers. It uses ultra reliable digital LED technology (pioneered by OKI and proven over more than 20 years) with fewer moving parts for exceptional durability. This robust, built-to-last design means that we are confident enough to give you our industry-leading three-year warranty at no extra cost.

Exceptional value for money

Not only does the B412dn give you much more for your money, but it’s surprisingly inexpensive to run. High yield consumable and built-in reliability all contribute to a low total cost of ownership.
